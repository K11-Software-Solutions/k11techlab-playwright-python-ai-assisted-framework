# Free Cloud Real Device Testing Options for Playwright

While there is no unlimited free cloud for real device testing, several providers offer free trials or open source credits:

## 1. BrowserStack
- **Free trial:** 100 minutes, limited devices
- **Supported devices:** Real Android phones/tablets, iPhones, iPads
- **Supported browsers:** Chrome, Firefox, Edge (Android); Safari (iOS)
- **Open Source Program:** Apply for extended free access if your project is open source
- **Education/Nonprofit:** Special programs available
- [Sign up](https://www.browserstack.com/users/sign_up)
- [Open Source Program](https://www.browserstack.com/open-source)
- [Docs: Playwright on Real Devices](https://www.browserstack.com/docs/automate/playwright/ios)

## 2. TestingBot
- **Free trial:** 100 minutes, limited devices
- **Supported devices:** Real Android and iOS devices
- **Supported browsers:** Chrome (Android), Safari (iOS)
- **Open Source Program:** [Apply here](https://testingbot.com/open-source)
- [Sign up](https://testingbot.com/signup)
- [Docs: Playwright on Real Devices](https://testingbot.com/support/playwright/real-devices.html)

## 3. LambdaTest
- **Free trial:** 60 minutes, limited devices
- **Supported devices:** Real Android and iOS devices
- **Supported browsers:** Chrome (Android), Safari (iOS)
- **Open Source Program:** [Apply here](https://www.lambdatest.com/open-source)
- [Sign up](https://accounts.lambdatest.com/register)
- [Docs: Playwright on Real Devices](https://www.lambdatest.com/support/docs/playwright-testing/)

## 4. Sauce Labs
- **Free trial:** 200 minutes, limited devices
- **Supported devices:** Real Android and iOS devices
- **Supported browsers:** Chrome (Android), Safari (iOS)
- **Open Source Program:** [Apply here](https://saucelabs.com/open-source)
- [Sign up](https://saucelabs.com/sign-up)
- [Docs: Playwright on Real Devices](https://docs.saucelabs.com/web-apps/automated-testing/playwright/)

## Notes
- All free trials require registration and are time/concurrency limited.
- For open source, education, or nonprofit projects, apply for their special programs for more free minutes.
- For continuous or production use, a paid plan is required.
